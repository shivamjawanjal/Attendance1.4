About GPJ-Attendance

Welcome to the GPJ-Attendance system! This application is designed to streamline the attendance tracking process for computer classes, ensuring that teachers can easily manage and record attendance for various classes such as CO-3K, CO-4K, CO-5I, and CO-6I.

Our platform is built with simplicity and efficiency in mind, allowing teachers to quickly add attendance records, view class information, and stay organized with minimal effort. Each class section provides a dedicated space for adding daily attendance, subject details, and class codes.

At GPJ-Attendance, we believe that technology can greatly enhance the educational experience by reducing administrative burdens and giving educators more time to focus on teaching. We're committed to providing a user-friendly and reliable tool that helps schools and institutions run more smoothly.

If you have any questions or need assistance, please don't hesitate to contact our support team. We're here to help!
